Impact of formally discussing mental health in the workplace

Coworker Comfort: Percentage of employees willing to discuss mental health with coworkers

Superviser Comfort: Percentage of employees willing to discuss mental health with supervisers

Seriousness: Percentage of employees who feel their employer takes mental health as seriously as physical health

Consequences: Percentage of employees who do not feel like discussing their mental illness has negative consequences

Response: Percentage of employees who do not feel like employees were responded to badly because of a mental health condition
